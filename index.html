Our Story

Olonkar (অলঙ্কার) is a passion project born out of love for traditional Bengali jewelry. Founded by Archisman and Ritisa, we believe every piece of jewelry tells a story — of craftsmanship, culture, and care.

Every piece in our collection is handmade, ensuring that when you wear Olonkar, you carry something truly unique and personal.
